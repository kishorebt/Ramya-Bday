ABOUT RAMYA

Welcome All. Hope you know this Angel very well. Adding few words about her !!!

On 13th July 1990, midst of heavy rainfall an cute little angel stepped out in this heavenly world to make this world even most beautiful ☺ .The cute little angel was named Ramya . Meaning of Ramya is Delightful, It is true too. She brought a joy of leap in their family and many people are thanking god for sending such an princess as their friend. She is Humble at heart, very polite girl, Pure thinking, peaceful, calm and soft spoken in nature.
She is an easy friend, with lot's of care, affection and love !!! she is very fun loving person where all her friends wants to hang out with her. She is very dedicated and sincere in her work and affectionate Friend for all, very concerned about the person she is around with and over all she is an amazing and awesome human being in this world where everyone love to be with her. we thank their parents for giving such a gem in our precious life, also we thank each and every person in her life who gave this gem a perfect shape and thank you ! you are a precious gift for everyone in their own life, we love to preserve you forever ♥♥♥
